Who are we

A diverse collective who ran a space like this for 5 years.

We're part of a network of dozens of independent cultural spaces, community groups, artists, organisers, city planners and we know there is a crying need for space like this

How we'll run the space

Creative collaboration and exhibition space
Event space
Vegan Cafe

We will provide the spatial infrastructure for a wide section of Dublin's community inviting them to an engaging open safe and affordable space for them to do their work.

How we'll fund it all

For startup costs we have access to savings, loans and grants. We have a community shares crowdfunding campaign ready to lauch.

For running costs we operate on a subscription-membership model and pay-per-use donation model for space users.

We intend to be a sustainably self-funded community space from the start.
